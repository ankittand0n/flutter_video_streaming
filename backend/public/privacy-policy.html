Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Namkeen TV ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Namkeen TV mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App, and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account information: email address and password (stored as a secure hash) when you register.
  • Usage data: watch history, watchlist items, and content ratings you submit within the App.
  • Playback position: stored locally on your device so you can resume where you left off.
  • Device information: operating system version and device type, collected anonymously for compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide personalised content recommendations based on your watchlist and ratings.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the App.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses TMDB (The Movie Database) to display movie metadata (titles, posters, descriptions). TMDB's privacy policy is available at themoviedb.org/privacy-policy. We do not share your personal information with TMDB.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date above. You are advised to review this page periodically.
